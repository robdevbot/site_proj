French

French is a beautiful and historic language. It is spoken by over 200 million people worldwide, with speakers concentrated in Western Europe, Africa, Southeast Asia, and Canada.

French and English

I began studying French because I enjoy etymology, the study of word origins. Many words in English come from French.

Here are a few French words commonly seen in English usage:

  • Bourgeois
  • Souvenir
  • Mirage
  • Cul-de-Sac
  • Encore
  • Genre
Some other words, like coup or entree, are used in English but have different meanings than their French counterparts.
